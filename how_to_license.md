# How to License Your Contribution

Thank you for your interest in contributing to this project! Please follow these steps to ensure your contribution is properly licensed:

1. **Sign the Salesforce Contributor License Agreement (CLA):**
   - Visit [https://cla.salesforce.com/](https://cla.salesforce.com/) and follow the instructions to sign the CLA.

2. **Include License Headers (if applicable):**
   - For new files, include the appropriate license header as specified in the project documentation.

3. **Submit Your Contribution:**
   - Open a pull request with a clear description of your changes.

4. **Wait for Review:**
   - A project maintainer will review your contribution and ensure all licensing requirements are met.

If you have any questions, please contact the maintainers or email [oss-questions@salesforce.com](mailto:oss-questions@salesforce.com).
